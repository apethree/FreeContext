Get up and running with OneShot in a few steps.

<Steps>

1. **Download the desktop app**

   Download the latest version from the [releases page](https://github.com/capzeroai/one-shot/releases).

2. **Sign in**

   OneShot uses Clerk for authentication. Sign in with your email or Google account.

3. **Connect a provider**

   Go to **Settings → Providers** and add your API key for at least one provider:
   - OpenAI (`sk-...`)
   - Anthropic (`sk-ant-...`)
   - Gemini (Google AI Studio key or OAuth)

4. **Start a chat**

   Click **New Session** and select your provider. Type your first message and hit Enter.

5. **Connect to cloud (optional)**

   To sync sessions across devices or enable channel support, go to **Settings → Cloud** and sign in. Your sessions will sync automatically.

</Steps>
